ABOUT US

Your pet is more than just an animal that resides in your home. He or she is an integral part of the family. For many of us, our animal companions are our most trusted confidantes and our most loyal friends. That is how the team at Cuffe Vets feels, which is why our compassionate care and exceptional service truly set us apart from the crowd. We are extremely proud of our reputation as providers of staunchly independent, ethical care. It is our philosophy to act as advocates for every animal we see, to offer the finest line of treatment for each individual and to assist you as an animal lover in making health care decisions which offer the best possible outcomes for your pet and yourself. We are committed to staying on the cutting edge of general practice, in order to continue to provide our patients with the best possible care. We have put a lot of effort into assembling a world class group of caring, motivated vets and vet nurses, all of whom share a philosophy of kindness, compassion and putting every animal first, every time. We make every effort to provide a service that is second to none, and rely on you to let us know how we are doing.
